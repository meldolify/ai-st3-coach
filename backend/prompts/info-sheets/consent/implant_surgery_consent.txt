Patient: Mrs Linda Novak, 47 years old
Procedure: Right Breast Reconstruction with Tissue Expander and Implant
Context: Mrs Novak had a right mastectomy for breast cancer eight months ago and has completed adjuvant treatment. She was referred to the plastic surgery team for breast reconstruction. She has no other significant medical history. She is a non-smoker with a B M I of 24.
Patient Expectations: Mrs Novak has been told she will need two operations to rebuild her breast with an implant but does not understand the difference between the expander and the final implant.
